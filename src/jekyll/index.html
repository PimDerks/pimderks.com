I'm a front-end developer from Nijmegen in the south-east of The Netherlands. I love to work with, discuss, tweet and write about HTML, CSS, JavaScript and other related stuff.

I've been building things for the internet since late 1998. Although I initially started as a webdesigner who also did a bit of HTML and CSS, but quite early on I felt that it was a waste of time making pictures of website. So gradually I shifted more and more to just front-end development. That's what I've been doing for the last six or seven years. Read more about me.
